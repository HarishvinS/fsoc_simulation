Hey! My name's Harishvin Sasikumar and I'm the highschool student who built this tool! I call it LightLink and it's designed to help with the deployment of Free Space Optical Communication (FSOC) systems. The simulation and optimization tools help engineers and researchers predict link performance, optimize deployment configurations, and accelerate the deployment of high-speed optical wireless communication in various environments.

Using atmospheric modeling and machine learning, LightLink analyzes environmental conditions, terrain factors, and system parameters to provide accurate performance predictions and deployment recommendations. Whether you're planning urban deployments through fog and atmospheric turbulence or rural long-distance links, LightLink provides the insights you need.
